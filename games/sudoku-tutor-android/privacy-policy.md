## Full Privacy Policy

### 1. Introduction

Welcome to Sudoku Tutor ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

This policy complies with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - California, USA
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - USA
- Google Play Developer Program Policies

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Information Collected Automatically

**Analytics Data (via Firebase Analytics)**
- App opens and session duration
- Screen views and navigation patterns
- Feature usage (e.g., hint usage, techniques learned)
- Device type, operating system version
- Country/region (not precise location)
- App version and crash-free statistics

**Crash Reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)**
- Stack traces and error logs
- Device state at time of crash
- Device model and OS version
- App version

**Advertising Data (via Google AdMob)**
- Advertising ID (can be reset in device settings)
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
- General location (country-level for ad relevance)

#### 2.2 Information You Provide

**Google Play Games (Optional)**
- If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games:
  - Player ID (anonymized identifier)
  - Display name (for leaderboards)
  - Achievement and leaderboard data

**Note:** We do NOT collect:
- Name, email address, or phone number
- Precise location or GPS data
- Contacts, photos, or files
- Payment information (handled by Google Play)

### 3. How We Use Your Information

| Data Type       | Purpose                                   | Legal Basis (GDPR)      |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------|-------------------------|
| Analytics       | Improve app features and user experience  | Legitimate interest     |
| Crash reports   | Fix bugs and improve stability            | Legitimate interest     |
| Advertising ID  | Show relevant ads, measure ad performance | Consent                 |
| Play Games data | Enable leaderboards and achievements      | Contract (your request) |

We do NOT sell your personal data.

### 7. Your Rights

#### For All Users:
- **Access:** Request what data we have about you
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data
- **Opt-out:** Disable personalized ads in device settings

#### Additional Rights for EU/EEA Residents (GDPR):
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent:** For consent-based processing

#### Additional Rights for California Residents (CCPA):
- **Know:** What personal information we collect
- **Delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Opt-out:** Of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Non-discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### 8. How to Exercise Your Rights

**Reset Advertising ID:**
- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID

**Opt Out of Personalized Ads:**
- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

**Delete Play Games Data:**
- Visit: https://play.google.com/settings
- Select "Delete Play Games account and data"

**Contact Us:**
- Email: [YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL]
- Subject: "Privacy Request - Sudoku Tutor"
- Include: Your request type and any relevant details

We will respond within:
- 30 days for GDPR requests
- 45 days for CCPA requests

### 9. Children's Privacy

Sudoku Tutor is rated for ages 7+ (PEGI) / E10+ (ESRB).

**COPPA Compliance:**
