Full Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sudoku Tutor ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

This policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - California, USA
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - USA
  • Google Play Developer Program Policies

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

Analytics Data (via Firebase Analytics)

  • App opens and session duration
  • Screen views and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage (e.g., hint usage, techniques learned)
  • Device type, operating system version
  • Country/region (not precise location)
  • App version and crash-free statistics

Crash Reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)

  • Stack traces and error logs
  • Device state at time of crash
  • Device model and OS version
  • App version

Advertising Data (via Google AdMob)

  • Advertising ID (can be reset in device settings)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • General location (country-level for ad relevance)

2.2 Information You Provide

Google Play Games (Optional)

  • If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games:
    • Player ID (anonymized identifier)
    • Display name (for leaderboards)
    • Achievement and leaderboard data

Note: We do NOT collect:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Precise location or GPS data
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Payment information (handled by Google Play)

Delete Play Games Data:

  • Visit: https://play.google.com/settings
  • Select "Delete Play Games account and data"
